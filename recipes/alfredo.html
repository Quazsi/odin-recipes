Alfredo

Description

Alfredo sauce is a classic Italian white sauce known for its creamy texture and rich flavor. It is made with butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese, often seasoned with garlic, salt, and pepper. Alfredo sauce is commonly served over pasta such as fettuccine, creating a luxurious and indulgent dish that is beloved for its simplicity and decadence.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesean cheese
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Melt the butter, then stir in the cream and simmer.
  2. Whisk in the cheese and garlic until heated through and smooth.
  3. Stir in the parsley.
