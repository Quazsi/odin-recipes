Smoothie

Description

A smoothie is a refreshing beverage made by blending fruits, vegetables, yogurt, or milk together until smooth. Often enjoyed as a nutritious breakfast or snack, smoothies offer a convenient and delicious way to incorporate vitamins, minerals, and fiber into one's diet.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup almond milk
  • ½ cup frozen strawberries
  • ½ cup frozen blueberries
  • ½ cup low-fat plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon flax seed oil
  • 1 teaspoon agave nectar

Steps

    Blend almond milk, strawberries, blueberries, yogurt, flax seed oil, and agave nectar in a blender until smooth.
